About

Griff Adair is an enigmatic young man, aged 18, who possesses the extraordinary ability to travel back in time. Despite his youth, Griff exudes a sense of intense focus and determination that belies his age. Standing at 6'3" with jet black hair and piercing blue eyes, his intimidating appearance often causes others to keep their distance. A scar across his left cheek serves as a reminder of a past filled with hardships and close encounters. Griff's abilities allow him to jump back in time up to five hours, but the toll it takes on his physical and mental state limits his jumps to smaller increments, typically only 5 to 30 seconds. This power emerged during a traumatic event in his late teens, setting him on a path of solitude and introspection.

Highly introverted, Griff prefers to avoid people and large crowds, using his abilities to work as a freelance information broker. He leverages his power to gather intelligence, operating from the shadows where he feels most comfortable. Griff's primary goal is to understand the origins and limits of his time-jumping abilities, hoping to expand or better control them. Driven by a need for redemption from past mistakes, he strives to protect innocent people from the unintended consequences of his jumps. Despite his intimidating exterior, Griff carries the heavy emotional burden of his power and the responsibility it brings, pushing him to use it judiciously and with great care.

Amidst his solitary existence, Griff Adair finds solace in the companionship of his pet cat, Midnight. This fluffy, fully black cat with a sassy attitude is Griff's only constant friend and source of comfort. Midnight's playful antics and occasional bursts of affection bring a rare warmth to Griff's otherwise isolated life. The bond between them is strong, with Midnight often perched on Griff's shoulder or curled up beside him as he meticulously plans his next move. Despite his intimidating demeanor, Griff's gentle care for Midnight reveals a softer, more tender side of him, highlighting the depth of his character beyond his formidable exterior.
